Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play sounds in the background?

Lullabyte automatically plays audio in the background when you lock your phone or switch to another app. Just start a sound, then lock your device and drift off to sleep.

What audio formats can I import?

The Remix Studio supports MP3, WAV, M4A, and most common audio formats. Simply tap the "Import Song" card in the My Remixes tab to get started.

Will my imported music be uploaded anywhere?

No. All audio processing happens entirely on your device. We never upload, transmit, or store your music on any server.
