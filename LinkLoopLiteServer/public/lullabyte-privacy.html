Privacy Policy

Lullabyte · Last updated: February 23, 2026

1. Overview

Lullabyte ("the App") is a sleep sounds and music remix application developed by VibeCMD LLC. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data. Lullabyte is designed to work primarily on-device.

  • No account required. You do not need to create an account or provide any personal information to use Lullabyte.
  • No audio data transmitted. All audio — including imported songs and generated sleep sounds — is processed entirely on your device. Nothing is uploaded to our servers.
  • Subscription data. If you subscribe to Lullabyte Pro, payment is processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. We receive anonymized subscription status through RevenueCat to verify your Pro entitlement.
  • Crash reports & analytics. We may collect anonymous crash reports and basic usage analytics (e.g., which sounds are most popular) to improve the app. This data is not linked to your identity.

3. Audio Files You Import

When you import audio files using the Remix Studio feature:

  • Files are accessed from your device's local storage or iCloud Drive.
  • Audio is processed entirely on-device.
  • We do not copy, upload, or transmit your audio files.
  • You retain full ownership and responsibility for any audio you import.

4. Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat — Manages subscription entitlements. See RevenueCat Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store — Handles payments and provides anonymized analytics. See Apple Privacy Policy.
  • Expo/EAS Updates — Delivers over-the-air app updates. No personal data is shared.

5. Children's Privacy

Lullabyte does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and does not require any personal data to function.

6. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing to retain or delete. Subscription status is managed by Apple and RevenueCat.

7. Your Rights

You may request information about any data associated with your use of the app. Since we collect no personal data, such requests will typically confirm that no data is held.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
