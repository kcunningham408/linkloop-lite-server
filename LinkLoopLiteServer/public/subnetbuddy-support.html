Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does SubnetBuddy need an internet connection?

A: No. SubnetBuddy works completely offline. All calculations are performed on your device with zero network calls.

Q: Do I need to create an account?

A: No. SubnetBuddy requires no login, registration, or account of any kind. Just open the app and start calculating.

Q: What subnet sizes are supported?

A: SubnetBuddy supports all IPv4 CIDR prefix lengths from /0 to /32.

Q: Does SubnetBuddy support IPv6?

A: Not yet. SubnetBuddy currently supports IPv4 only. IPv6 support may be added in a future update.

Q: Is SubnetBuddy free?

A: Yes. SubnetBuddy is completely free with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscriptions.

Q: Does SubnetBuddy scan my network?

A: No. SubnetBuddy is a calculator only. It does not connect to, scan, or interact with any networks.
