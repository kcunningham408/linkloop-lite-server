SubnetBuddy — Privacy Policy

Effective: February 24, 2026

Overview

SubnetBuddy is a fully offline subnet calculator and network planning utility built by VibeCMD LLC. Your privacy is simple: we collect nothing.

Data Collection

SubnetBuddy does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever. The app performs all calculations entirely on your device with zero network calls.

No Account Required

SubnetBuddy does not require any account creation, login, or registration. There are no usernames, passwords, or email addresses involved.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics frameworks, tracking pixels, advertising SDKs, or third-party data collection tools. No usage data leaves your device.

No Network Access

SubnetBuddy makes zero network requests. It does not connect to any servers, APIs, or external services. All subnet calculations, mask conversions, VLAN planning, and IP visualization happen locally on your device.

No Third-Party Services

SubnetBuddy does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or data brokers.

Children's Privacy

SubnetBuddy does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. It is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Since we collect no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
