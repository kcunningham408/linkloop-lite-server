## Description (4,000 characters max)
LinkLoop T1D is the all-in-one personal wellness journal built for people living with Type 1 Diabetes and the families who support them. Designed by a T1D dad, LinkLoop brings glucose tracking, mood journaling, AI-powered insights, and Care Circle sharing into a single, beautiful app.

GLUCOSE TRACKING
• Log glucose readings manually with values, trends, and notes
• Connect your Dexcom CGM for automatic real-time glucose data
• View 24-hour glucose charts with color-coded ranges
• Track time in range with clear visual breakdowns

AI-POWERED INSIGHTS
• Get personalized glucose summaries powered by AI
• Discover patterns and trends across your readings
• Receive daily motivational messages tailored to your mood and glucose data
• AI correlates your mood notes with glucose patterns to find what matters

MOOD & NOTES JOURNAL
• Log how you're feeling with 8 mood options — from Great to Anxious
• Add optional notes about your day (pizza night, stressful exam, feeling off)
• View weekly mood frequency stats
• Your mood data feeds into AI insights so patterns emerge over time

ACHIEVEMENTS & BADGES
• Earn 25 badges across 5 categories: Streaks, Milestones, Consistency, Explorer, and Community
• Track your progress with a visual badge grid
• Celebrate wins like 10 readings in a row, 80% time in range, or logging your first mood
• Check for new badges anytime

CARE CIRCLE
• Invite family members and caregivers to your Care Circle via invite code or SMS text
• Share glucose data in real-time with the people who matter
• In-app group chat keeps everyone coordinated
• Smart alerts notify caregivers when readings need attention

SUPPLY TRACKING
• Track insulin, test strips, sensors, and other T1D supplies
• Set quantities and get low-stock reminders
• Never run out of critical supplies again

BUILT WITH CARE
• Dark theme with T1D blue accents — easy on the eyes day and night
• Not a medical device — LinkLoop is a personal wellness journal
• Your data is encrypted and never sold
• Built by a dad whose daughter lives with T1D every single day

LinkLoop T1D is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider for medical decisions.
